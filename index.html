Company Vision

Our mission is to lead the transformation in AI by developing autonomous model maintenance agents. These agents ensure continuous improvement and adaptability, positioning SHARP MACHINE as the pioneer of the Sharp Machine era.

Founder's Story

In 2021, Deep Mukhopadhyay was struck by a statistic: 85% of AI systems fail in real-world applications. Inspired by this challenge and insights from AI expert Peter Norvig, Deep left his job to develop a solution. After two years of dedicated research, the breakthrough SHARP technology was born, laying the foundation for SHARP MACHINE, Inc.
