Technology

Our technology addresses the lifecycle of a model beyond its training stage. SHARP technology continuously learns and improves from new data, rather than remaining static after training. This key innovation ensures models are robust and reliable in dynamic environments.

SHARP acts as an "autonomous model maintenance agent," intelligently sharpening models as new data arrives. This major shift from static "best trained" models to "self-corrective" predictive agents significantly reduces costs and time while enhancing safety.
