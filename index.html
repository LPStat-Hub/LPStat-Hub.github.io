SHARP AI Service Deployment Strategies

  1. Cloud API: SHARP AI offers RESTful APIs for easy integration of model sharpening capabilities into customer applications.
  2. On-Premises: Delivered as a software package installed on the customer's local servers or data centers. Ensures all data processing happens within the customer's secure environment.
